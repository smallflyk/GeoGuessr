About GeoGuessr

GeoGuessr is an engaging online geography game that challenges players to identify locations around the world based solely on panoramic Street View images. Originally created in 2013, it has become a popular educational tool and entertainment platform for geography enthusiasts of all ages.

With GeoGuessr, you can test and improve your geographical knowledge, discover new places, and learn about different cultures and landscapes from the comfort of your home.
