About GeoGuessr

GeoGuessr is an exciting geography game that challenges your knowledge of the world. When playing, you'll be dropped at random locations across the globe using Street View technology, and your task is to figure out where you are by looking at your surroundings.

The closer your guess is to the actual location, the more points you earn. This game provides a fun and educational way to discover new places and test your geography skills!

How to Play GeoGuessr

1

Observe

Look at the street view imagery and search for clues like road signs, landscapes, architecture, or vegetation.

2

Navigate

Use the controls to move around and explore the area to gather more information about your location.

3

Make Your Guess

Place your marker on the world map where you think you are. The closer you are, the more points you'll score in GeoGuessr!

Tips for Success

Look for Language Clues

Road signs, advertisements, and storefronts can give you hints about the country or region in GeoGuessr.

Check the Environment

Vegetation, climate, terrain, and architectural styles can narrow down your location.

Traffic Rules

Notice which side of the road cars are driving on - this can eliminate many countries when playing.

Study the Sun

The position of the sun can give you clues about whether you're in the northern or southern hemisphere.
